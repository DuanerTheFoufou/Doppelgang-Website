Privacy Policy
Last Updated: November 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Doppelgang ("we", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Username, name, age, location, and bio provided during sign-up.
  • Photos: The photos you upload to your profile.
  • Face Data: We use facial analysis technology to detect facial features in your photos.

3. How We Use Face Data

  • Purpose: We use Face Data solely to calculate a "Match Score" between you and other users (including celebrities).
  • Processing: When you upload a photo, our server analyzes the facial geometry to create a mathematical representation (a "vector embedding").
  • Storage: We store this vector embedding in our secure database. We do not store the raw biometric scan of your face, only the mathematical representation.
  • Sharing: We do not share, sell, or trade your Face Data with any third parties.

4. Data Retention & User Rights

Your data is retained only as long as you have an active account. You may delete your account and all associated data (including photos and Face Data) at any time by going to Profile > Settings > Delete Account. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our servers.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@doppelgang.app for any privacy-related inquiries.
