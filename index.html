Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

1. Introduction & Collection

Doppelgang ("we", "our") respects your privacy. We collect Account Information (Username, age, bio) and Face Data (via photo uploads) to operate the service.

2. How We Use Face Data

  • Purpose: We use Face Data solely to calculate a "Match Score" between you and other users (including celebrities).
  • Processing: When you upload a photo, our server analyzes the facial geometry to create a mathematical representation (a "vector embedding").
  • Storage: We store this vector embedding in our secure database. We do not store the raw biometric scan of your face, only the mathematical representation.
  • Sharing: We do not share, sell, or trade your Face Data with any third parties.

3. Data Retention & Rights

Your data is retained only as long as you have an active account. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by going to Profile > Settings > Delete Account.
